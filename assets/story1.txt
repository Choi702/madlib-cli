Make Me A Video Game!

I the panda and ninja enforcers have trained my fierce sister and plan to steal her diamond ninja sword!

What are a panda and backpacking ninja enforcers to do? Before you can help fuzz, you'll have to collect the deadly scorpion and fire breathing ants that open up the 2021 worlds connected to A phone booth. There are 1500 jump ropes and 46 snails in the game, along with hundreds of other goodies for you to find.
